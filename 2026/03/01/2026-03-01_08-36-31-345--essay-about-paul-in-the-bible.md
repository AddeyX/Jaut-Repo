Ephesians is Paul's letter to the Ephesians, specifically the followers of Christ in Ephesus where Paul went to spread the gospel. Ephesus , like many regions was plagued by sinful nature like sexual immortality and idolatry of arise Gods.

Paul's letter to Ephesus starts off with a brief greeting and Paul giving glory to God "“Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who has blessed us in Christ with every spiritual blessing in the heavenly places,” ‭‭Ephesians‬ ‭1‬:‭3‬ ‭ESV‬‬". Paul in his letter emphasizes how we , believers were "predestined" to be followers of Christ and our inheritance secured by the Holy Spirit within us. 

Paul in his letter talks about how we were all dead due to our sin , but through Gods great grace , mercy and his love for us he made us alive through Christ. It's not by our own doing , its due oto grace through faith . All believers, sons and daughters are on equal footing, Gentiles or jew are part of the same body, the body of christ.

Paul writes about the mystery of the gospel, not mystery to deceive but God choosing to withold the truth until we are ready. The mystery of the gospel was revealed when Jesus arrived. He is the way. We also see Paul pray for spiritual strength , Ephesians 3:17"so that Christ may dwell in your hearts thorugh faith-that you\,being rooted and grounded in love" may have strength...".
 
Paul also writes that as christians we ought to live as imitators of christ, and take no part in unfruitful  works of darkness but instead EXPOSE them. Paul also speak of Husbands and Wife and how they should interact with one another, specifically how we should posture ourselfs. God calls wives to submit to their husbands and husbands love your wives as Christ love the church. 

Paul continues on with more guidance on how children should obey their parents but Fathers/parents should not provoke their children . Ephesians has a lot of wisdom that we as christians should cling to as we walk through our lives. At the end of the day, no matter what we need to remember who we belong to.
