Phase 1: The "Foundation" (Weeks 1-2) The Chassis: Build your "Master SvelteKit Template." It should include:  Pre-configured Tailwind CSS themes.  Cloudflare Web Analytics integration.  A "Contact Us" component ready for Cloudflare Pages Functions.  The Content Gateway: Set up a Tally.so form. This is your "Order Form." It should require:  Brand colors/Logo.  5 High-res images.  "About Us" intent phrases for the AI.  The Billing Link: Set up a Stripe Product for your "Partner" tier. No project starts until the first payment is hit.  Phase 2: The "Lead Magnet" Sprint (Weeks 3-4) Targeting: Identify 20 businesses in York or your PSU network with "broken" or non-existent sites.  The AI Mockup: Use a tool like Vercel’s v0 or a custom Svelte-generator to create a single-page landing preview.  Outreach: Send a screen recording (Loom) or high-res screenshot.  "Hey [Name], I'm a PSU grad and Process Engineer in York. I noticed your site is [slow/missing]. I spent 15 mins building this Svelte-powered version that loads 4x faster. Check it out."  Phase 3: The "Fulfilment Factory" (Ongoing) The 2-Hour Build:  Hour 1: Use AI to turn their Tally.so inputs into Svelte components and copy.  Hour 2: Manual polish, SEO metadata, and Cloudflare deployment.  The Hand-off: Send the client their "Success Kit" (Login to their knowledge base, analytics link, and instructions on how to request changes).
