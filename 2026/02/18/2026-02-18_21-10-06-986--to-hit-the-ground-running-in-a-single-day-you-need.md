To hit the ground running in a single day, you need to transition from "Developer" to "Business Owner." As a Process Engineer, you know that a successful line requires a stable Master Template and a clear Input/Output flow.  Here is your 1-day, high-impact "Svelte-Scale" sprint.  Morning (08:00 – 11:00): Build the "Chassis" You shouldn't be writing npm create svelte@latest for every client. You need a production-ready boilerplate.  Task: Create a private GitHub repository called svelte-client-chassis.  Must-Haves: * SEO: Pre-configured Meta tags (OpenGraph, Twitter cards).  Analytics: A single environment variable for your Cloudflare/Plausible ID.  Components: A high-end "Contractor Hero," "Testimonial Grid," and "Service List."  Forms: A working contact form leveraging Cloudflare Pages Functions (so you don't need a backend).  Goal: By 11 AM, you should be able to clone this repo and have a professional site structure running in 5 minutes.  Mid-Day (11:00 – 13:00): The Onboarding & Legal "Gateway" Eliminate the back-and-forth emails. If the client doesn't provide the info, you don't start the work.  Task 1: The Tally Form: Create a "New Client Discovery" form. Force them to upload their logo, choose a primary color, and provide 3–5 bullet points for their AI-generated copy.  Task 2: The Service Agreement: Draft a 1-page "Mutual Success" PDF. Keep it simple: “I build it, you own it, I host it. Revisions capped at 2 rounds.”  Task 3: Lemon Squeezy: Set up your store and create your two tiers. Link your Service Agreement to the "Thank You" page or the receipt.  Afternoon (13:00 – 16:00): The "Lead Magnet" Factory Now you need the "hooks" to catch your first 5 clients.  Task: Identify 5 contractors or coaches in the York/PSU network who have a "Google Business Profile" but either no website or a very old one.  The Mockup: Spend 20 minutes for each prospect. Use your Svelte Chassis to swap out the logo and colors to match their brand.  The Loom: Record a 60-second video for each.  "Hey [Name], I noticed your site takes 6 seconds to load. I rebuilt a version of your homepage that loads in 0.5 seconds. Check it out."  Evening (16:00 – 18:00): The Outreach Blitz Hit the "Send" button. Do not overthink the copy.  Task: Send your 5 Loom videos/Screenshots via Email or LinkedIn.  The "PSU Bonus": Mention your Penn State background—it’s the easiest way to bypass the "random solicitor" filter.  The Follow-up: Set a reminder for 48 hours from now to follow up with anyone who hasn't replied.  The Day 1 Completion Checklist [ ] The Chassis: GitHub repo is live with a "Contractor" and "Coach" theme.  [ ] The Gateway: Tally form is ready to collect assets.  [ ] The Wallet: Lemon Squeezy is ready to accept payments and handle taxes.  [ ] The Leads: 5 personalized mockups are sent to 5 real people.
