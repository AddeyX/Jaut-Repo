Pro-Tip: The "Waitlist" Psychological Trigger If someone says "Yes" today, don't say "I can start right now." Tell them: "I’m currently finishing up a process optimization project, but I can slot you in for my next 2-hour 'Sprint Build' this Saturday." This makes you look like a busy professional, not a desperate freelancer.
