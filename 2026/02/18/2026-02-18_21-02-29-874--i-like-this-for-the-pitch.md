Here is a 45-second pitch tailored for a local contractor or coach.

The "Svelte-Process" Pitch
"Hi, I’m Emmanuel. I’m a Process Engineer and PSU alum here in York.

I’ve noticed that most local contractors and coaches are actually losing money because of their websites. They’re either using bloated builders like Wix that take forever to load on a phone, or they’re stuck ‘fiddling’ with tech instead of being on the job site.

I build what I call 'High-Performance Digital Assets.' Using the same framework companies like Apple and Disney use, I build custom sites that load instantly and rank higher on Google.

But more importantly, I act as your Digital Partner. I handle the domain, the hosting, and the professional email for you. You get the social proof of a high-end agency site without the high-end headache.

I actually took the liberty of running a speed test on your current setup and mocked up a high-speed Svelte version for you—do you have a minute to see the difference?"

Why this works:
The Authority: "Process Engineer" tells them you're methodical and won't flake.

The Villain: You identify the problem (bloated, slow sites) without sounding like a jerk.

The Solution: You position yourself as a "Partner," not a "Vendor."

The Hook: You end with a "Gift" (the mockup) rather than a "Request" (the sale).

Pro-Tips for Delivery:
Don’t say "Svelte" or "Cloudflare": To a contractor, those are just confusing tech words. Use words like "Instant," "Secure," and "Custom-coded."

Focus on the Phone: Contractors live on their phones. Emphasize how fast the site is on mobile.

The "Loom" Alternative: If you can't meet them in person, record a 60-second Loom video showing their current site's slow mobile score vs. your lightning-fast Svelte mockup. It’s an unbeatable lead magnet.

Would you like me to create a "Day 1 Checklist" for your Svelte "Factory" so you can get that first mockup built in record time?
